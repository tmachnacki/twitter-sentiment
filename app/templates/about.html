Overview

Our web application uses a pre-trained BERT-base model from PySentimiento to analyze and determine the sentiment from retreived tweets. The model used in our application also utilizes Twitter-specific tokenization to pre-process tweets for better accuracy metrics. For detailed descriptions of our approach, methodology, and experience in working with different models and classifiers please view our written report and poster.

Usage

We ask that you do not spam becuase we are limited to 500,000 tweet retrievals per month and 25 requests per 15 minutes due to our basic Twitter API license.
1. Enter your query

Search for a topic, brand, company, product, etc. (i.e. Thursday Night Football, Coke, Amazon) by typing in the search bar.

2. Select a date range

Choose over which dates you want to analyze tweets from by selecting a "start" and "end" date. Our tool is limited to retrieving tweets from only the past 5 days due to our Twitter API license.

3. Select the amount of tweets per day to analyze

Use the slider to select the desired amount of tweets per day to analyze. We are limited to retrieving a relatively low number of tweets per day due to our API license. Note that selecting more tweets will result in slower results.

You can now get your results or reset the form if you are unhappy with your query parameters.
